Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to FXLotSize. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you share while using our web application and risk management tools.

2. Information Collection & Usage

FXLotSize is designed as a client-side calculator tool. All mathematical calculations, input parameters (balance, risk %, stop-loss levels), and configurations are performed locally within your web browser.

  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not require account registration, nor do we collect names, email addresses, or financial credentials.
  • No Saved Inputs: Your trading inputs remain in your browser session and are not transmitted or stored on any remote server.

3. Third-Party Services & Embeds

Our website utilizes third-party widgets and services for enhanced analytics and market insights, such as TradingView widgets and standard web analytics. These services may collect anonymous usage metrics or set cookies in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

4. Cookies

FXLotSize does not directly set tracking cookies. Any cookies used on the site are strictly necessary for core functionality or provided by third-party embeds (e.g., TradingView Economic Calendar).

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any modifications will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our Contact Page.
